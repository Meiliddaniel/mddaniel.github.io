Meili Daniel

Meili Daniel is from the coast of Mendocino and she is currently a student at the University of San Francisco majoring in design.

Growing up I have always love painting, crafting, and other forms of art. I am excited for design to be my new creative outlet and to gain a deeper understanding of how I can use visuals to communicate with an audience. I have experience with web design, logo design as well as the Adobe Suite which includes photoshop, illustrator and indesign. I enjoy mixing medias within my work and experimenting with how fine art can be incorporated into this technologically driven world. I hope to work at a company who encourages creative freedom, has strong morals and exposes me to new experiences and opportunities.
