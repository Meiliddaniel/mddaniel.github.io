Summer Time

Illustrator, 2023

Individual project done on Adobe Illustrator. I strive to capture the feeling of a tropical summer by using warm earthy tones. I like to play around with my line work and colors in my work. The purpose of this project was to reflect my artistic skills and to show the type of illustration I am interested in/capable of. I also used this as an opportunity to show my experience in Adobe Illustrator.

Jessica Hische

Illustrator, 2022

This icon is inspired by the graphic designer, Jessica Hische. Done on Adobe Illustrator. I strive to capture her use of bright colors and child like illustrations within the project. Jessica Hische is also well known for her intricate lettering which I represent on the side of the blocks which are her initials. The purpose of this project was to embody a female designer in an icon.
